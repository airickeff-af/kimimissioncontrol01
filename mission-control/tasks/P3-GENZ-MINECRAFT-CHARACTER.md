## CORE CONCEPT

### 1. Character Identity
- Minecraft-based avatar with Gen Z aesthetic (tech patterns, streaming colors, fitness accessories)
- **Autonomous/semi-autonomous system** responding to real-world health/financial inputs
- Visual representation of wellness vs. financial independence tension

### 2. Dual-Metric Tracking
| Health Metrics | Wealth Metrics |
|----------------|----------------|
| Physical wellness (exercise, sleep, nutrition) | Liquid assets (32% of Gen Z allocation) |
| Mental health (stress, therapy, mindfulness) | Investments (crypto, micro-investing) |
| Preventive care (checkups, wellness spending) | Income streams (gig work, side hustles) |
| Sleep quality | Debt obligations (student loans) |

### 3. Real-Time Feedback
- **Immediate:** Status effects, animations, sound cues
- **Short-term:** Appearance modifications, equipment access
- **Long-term:** Character scaling, box environment transformation
