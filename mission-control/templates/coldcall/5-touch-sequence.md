## Subject Line Best Practices

### âœ… What Works
- Specific numbers ($38.4B, 18.6M)
- Company names (theirs and yours)
- Questions (creates curiosity)
- "Re:" (implies ongoing conversation)
- Personalization (their name)
- Under 50 characters (mobile-friendly)

### âŒ What Doesn't
- ALL CAPS or excessive punctuation!!!
- Generic phrases ("Partnership opportunity")
- Misleading subject lines (damages trust)
- Too long (gets cut off on mobile)
- Spam triggers: "FREE", "URGENT", "ACT NOW"

### A/B Testing Framework

| Test Element | Variant A | Variant B |
|--------------|-----------|-----------|
| Length | Short (3-5 words) | Long (descriptive) |
| Personalization | With name | Without name |
| Question vs Statement | Question | Statement |
| Numbers | With stats | Without stats |
| Urgency | Time-sensitive | Evergreen |

## Timing & Cadence

### Recommended Sequence Timeline

| Touch | Day | Goal | If Response |
|-------|-----|------|-------------|
| 1 | Day 1 | Open conversation | Move to meeting booking |
| 2 | Day 4-5 | Bump to top | Move to meeting booking |
| 3 | Day 9-10 | Provide value | Move to meeting booking |
| 4 | Day 14-16 | Create urgency | Move to meeting booking |
| 5 | Day 21-23 | Close loop | Archive or re-engage later |

### Adjust Based On
- **Seniority:** C-level = longer intervals (they're busier)
- **Company size:** Enterprise = longer sales cycle
- **Seasonality:** Avoid major holidays, conference weeks
- **Your capacity:** Don't send more than you can follow up on

### Optimal Send Times

**Best Days:**
1. Wednesday (51.2% open rate)
2. Tuesday (48.5% open rate)
3. Thursday (47.8% open rate)

**Best Hours:**
1. 10:00 AM (54.1% open rate)
2. 9:00 AM (52.3% open rate)
3. 11:00 AM (51.8% open rate)

**Avoid:**
- Monday mornings (catch-up mode)
- Friday afternoons (weekend mode)
- Weekend (15-18% open rates)
