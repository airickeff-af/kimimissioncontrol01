```

           prepare a thread. Gary, what's the angle?"

Glasses: "Already on it. Pulling from 12 sources..."

Quill: "Give me 20 minutes with Glasses' research. I'll make it punchy."

Gary: "Angle it toward productivity gains. That's what's converting right now."
```

### Office Decor
- Large windows with city view
- Plants scattered around
- Whiteboards on walls
- Status display screens
- Comfortable lounge area
- Kitchenette with coffee

### Lighting
- Natural light from windows
- Desk lamps for focused work
