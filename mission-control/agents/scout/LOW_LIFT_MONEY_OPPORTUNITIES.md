## 1. Solana Ecosystem Airdrop Farming

### What It Is
Systematically interact with Solana DeFi protocols to qualify for token airdrops. 2025 is shaping up to be "Solana Airdrop Season 2" with multiple high-value drops expected.

### Time Required
- **Setup:** 2-3 hours to create wallets and fund with SOL
- **Ongoing:** 30-60 minutes weekly for interactions
- **Total commitment:** 2-4 hours/month

### Potential Earnings
- **Conservative:** $500-2,000 per successful airdrop
- **Aggressive:** $5,000-15,000+ for farming multiple protocols
- **2025 examples:** Previous Solana airdrops (Jupiter, Jito) ranged $1,000-50,000+

### How to Start
1. Create dedicated wallets (use Phantom or Solflare)
2. Fund with 5-10 SOL for gas + interactions
3. Target protocols with confirmed/expected airdrops:
   - **Drift** (perpetuals DEX) - Season 2 active
   - **Kamino** (lending) - Season 3 ongoing
   - **Sanctum** (liquid staking) - Season 2 expected
   - **Grass** (data layer) - Multiple seasons planned
   - **DeBridge** (cross-chain) - Token launch pending
4. Perform weekly swaps, provide liquidity, or stake
5. Track positions on [airdrops.io](https://airdrops.io) or [dropsearn.com](https://dropsearn.com)

### Why It's Low-Lift
- No capital at risk (just gas fees ~$0.01-0.50 per tx on Solana)
- Can automate with simple calendar reminders
- Multiple protocols = diversified shots on goal
- Your existing crypto knowledge gives you an edge

## 2. AI Automation Micro-Agency

### What It Is
Build simple AI automations for small businesses using no-code tools (Make, Zapier, n8n). Focus on repetitive tasks like lead follow-up, appointment scheduling, or content repurposing.

### Time Required
- **Setup:** 4-6 hours learning n8n or Make
- **Per client:** 3-5 hours initial build, 30 min/month maintenance
- **Scale:** Can handle 5-10 clients in 10-15 hours/week

### Potential Earnings
- **Per client:** $500-2,000 setup + $200-500/month retainer
- **With 5 clients:** $2,500-10,000 setup + $1,000-2,500/month recurring
- **First client potential:** Within 2-3 weeks

### How to Start
1. **Learn the stack** (2-3 days):
   - n8n (free self-hosted) or Make.com (visual builder)
   - OpenAI API integration
   - Basic CRM connections (Airtable, Notion, Google Sheets)
2. **Pick a niche** you understand:
   - Content creators (auto-repurpose long-form to shorts)
   - Coaches/consultants (automated booking + follow-up)
   - E-commerce (abandoned cart recovery, review requests)
3. **Build 2-3 demo workflows** for portfolio
4. **Outreach:** DM 20 prospects/day on X/Twitter or LinkedIn
5. **Offer:** "I save you 10 hours/week for $500/month"

### Why It's Low-Lift
- No-code tools = no programming required
- Workflows are reusable across clients
- Retainer model = predictable income
- Your Mission Control experience = instant credibility

### Why It's Low-Lift
- You already follow crypto/NFT markets

## 5. Crypto Staking & Restaking Yields

### What It Is
Earn passive income by staking ETH, SOL, and other assets. New "restaking" protocols (EigenLayer, Solayer) offer stacked yields on the same capital.

### Time Required
- **Setup:** 1-2 hours
- **Maintenance:** 30 minutes monthly
- **Total:** Nearly passive after setup

### Potential Earnings
- **Base staking:** 3-8% APY (ETH, SOL)
- **Restaking:** 8-20% APY (EigenLayer, Solayer)
- **With $50K:** $4,000-10,000/year passive
- **Airdrop bonus:** Restaking protocols often reward early users

### How to Start
1. **ETH restaking** (higher yields):
   - Stake ETH on Lido (stETH)
   - Deposit stETH into EigenLayer
   - Delegate to operators
   - Monitor for airdrops (Eigen token already launched, more coming)
2. **Solana restaking** (easier, lower fees):
   - Stake SOL with Sanctum (liquid staking)
   - Use Solayer for restaking
   - Earn double yield + potential airdrops
3. **Track yields:**
   - [StakeRewards](https://stakerewards.com)
   - [DeFiLlama Yields](https://yields.defillama.com)

### Why It's Low-Lift
- Set-and-forget income
- No active trading required
- Restaking = yield on top of yield
- Your existing crypto holdings can work harder
