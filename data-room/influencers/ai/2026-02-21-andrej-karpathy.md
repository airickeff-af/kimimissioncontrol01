## Profile
Andrej Karpathy is one of the most respected voices in artificial intelligence, known for his ability to make complex AI concepts accessible while maintaining technical depth. He is the founder of Eureka Labs, an AI-native education platform aimed at modernizing learning in the age of AI.

His career spans the most influential AI organizations in the world: he was a founding member at OpenAI (2015-2017), Director of AI at Tesla where he led the Autopilot computer vision team (2017-2022), and returned to OpenAI to work on GPT-4 and synthetic data generation (2023-2024).

## Recent Activity
- **Eureka Labs Launch (2024):** Founded Eureka Labs to build "a new kind of school that is AI native"—focused on reimagining education through AI
- **RLVR Insights (2025):** Recently shared insights on Reinforcement Learning from Verifiable Rewards (RLVR) emerging as a major new paradigm in LLM training
- **YouTube Education:** Maintains an active YouTube channel posting lectures on LLMs and AI fundamentals
- **Educational Content:** Regularly shares deep technical threads explaining AI concepts from neural network fundamentals to cutting-edge developments

## Key Insights
- **Educational Impact:** Created and taught CS231n at Stanford, the university's first deep learning course, which grew from 150 students (2015) to 750 (2017)
- **Technical Credibility:** Led Tesla's Autopilot computer vision team, handling all in-house data labeling, neural network training, and deployment on Tesla's custom inference chip
- **Open Source Contributions:** Created micrograd (tiny autograd engine) and ConvNetJS (deep learning library in JavaScript)
- **Communication Style:** Known for making complex AI concepts accessible without sacrificing technical accuracy
- **Industry Perspective:** Unique experience spanning OpenAI (twice), Tesla, Google Brain, Google Research, and DeepMind
