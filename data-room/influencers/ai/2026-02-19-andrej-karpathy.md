## Profile
Andrej Karpathy is one of the most influential voices in artificial intelligence, bridging cutting-edge research with accessible education. As the founder of Eureka Labs, he's pioneering AI-native education platforms that reimagine how people learn in the age of AI. His career spans founding team member at OpenAI, Director of AI at Tesla (leading Autopilot computer vision), and creator of Stanford's legendary CS231n deep learning course.

Karpathy is known for his ability to explain complex AI concepts with clarity and depth. His "Zero to Hero" neural networks series and LLM education content have educated millions. He holds a PhD from Stanford under Fei-Fei Li and has worked at Google Brain, DeepMind, and OpenAI across two separate stints.
