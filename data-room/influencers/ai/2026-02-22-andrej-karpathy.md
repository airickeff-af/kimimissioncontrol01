## Profile
Andrej Karpathy is a computer scientist, AI researcher, and founding member of OpenAI who later led Tesla's Autopilot program from 2017-2022. He is widely regarded as one of the most influential voices in AI, known for making complex concepts accessible through his educational content. In 2024, he founded Eureka Labs, an AI-native education company focused on building AI-powered teaching assistants.

His career spans the most important AI organizations: OpenAI (founding team), Tesla (Director of AI for Autopilot), and now Eureka Labs as founder. He holds a PhD from Stanford and has over 1.2 million YouTube subscribers for his AI tutorials.
