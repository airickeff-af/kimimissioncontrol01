## Profile
Michael Saylor is the Executive Chairman of Strategy (formerly MicroStrategy), the largest corporate holder of Bitcoin in the world. He has transformed his company into a Bitcoin treasury vehicle, holding over 3.2% of Bitcoin's total supply. Saylor is arguably the most influential institutional voice in crypto, with his tweets capable of moving billions in market cap within minutes.

His "Bitcoin for Corporations" initiative has convened CEOs, CFOs, and treasurers to educate them on Bitcoin as a treasury reserve asset. His conviction has inspired numerous public companies to add Bitcoin to their balance sheets.
