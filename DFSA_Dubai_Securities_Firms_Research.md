## Executive Summary

This report identifies **12 qualified DFSA-licensed securities firms** in Dubai's DIFC that hold licenses equivalent to HKSI Paper 2 (Advising on Securities) and/or HKSI Paper 3 (Dealing in Securities). The firms are categorized by their crypto/digital asset focus and regional relevance.

### DFSA License Categories Explained:
- **Category 4**: Advisory and arranging activities (equivalent to HKSI Paper 2 - Advising on Securities)
  - Advising on Financial Products
  - Arranging Deals in Investments
  - Arranging Credit & Advising on Credit
  
- **Category 5**: Islamic Finance (Shariah-compliant financing and assets)
  
- **Category 3A/3B/3C**: Dealing in Investments (equivalent to HKSI Paper 3 - Dealing in Securities)
  - Dealing in Investments as Agent
  - Dealing in Investments as Principal
