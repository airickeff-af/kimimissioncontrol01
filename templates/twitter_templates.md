## Character Limits

- **DM limit:** 10,000 characters (plenty of room)
- **But keep it short:** 2-3 sentences max for first contact
- **Twitter culture:** Casual, emoji-friendly, get to the point fast

## Best Practices

1. **Engage publicly first** when possible â€” warms up the DM
2. **Reference specific tweets** â€” shows you're paying attention
3. **Keep it casual** â€” Twitter is less formal than LinkedIn
4. **Don't be salesy** â€” "partnership" > "I want to sell you something"
5. **Use their language** â€” mirror their tone (professional vs. casual)
