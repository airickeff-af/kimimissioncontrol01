## Best Practices

### Subject Lines
- Keep under 50 characters (mobile-friendly)
- Avoid spam triggers: "FREE", "URGENT", "ACT NOW", excessive punctuation
- Personalization increases open rates by 26%
- Questions perform well
- "Re:" follow-ups get attention

### Body Copy
- **Length:** 100-150 words for first email
- **Paragraphs:** Max 2-3 lines each
- **Focus:** Lead with THEIR needs, not your product
- **CTA:** One clear ask
- **P.S.:** Use it ‚Äî people read PS lines

### Timing
- **First email:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am prospect's timezone
- **Follow-up:** 3-4 business days
- **Breakup:** 1 week after follow-up
- **Never:** Monday mornings, Friday afternoons

### Tracking
- Use email tracking (Hunter, Yesware, or similar)
- Note opens, clicks, and replies
- Adjust messaging based on engagement
